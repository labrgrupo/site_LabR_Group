About the Lab R Group



The LabR Group is a voluntary and non-profit collective, founded in 2021, composed of a variety of professionals who work directly and indirectly in the clinical laboratory sector in Brazil. These specialists focus on quality management, employing statistical tools that aid in this endeavor. The primary aim of the group is to implement digital transformation in quality tools, utilizing the R programming language, which is open source and free of charge.


Mission

  • To drive digital transformation in the quality management of clinical laboratories in Brazil, utilizing the open-source and free R programming language, and statistical tools to contribute to the continuous improvement of health services.


Vision

  • To be a pioneering group in initiating and fostering a movement towards digital transformation in the quality management of clinical laboratories in Brazil, contributing to improved healthcare outcomes.


Objectives

  • Implement digital transformation in quality tools, utilizing the R programming language, which is open source and free of charge.

  • Promote the training and continuous development of professionals in the field of quality management and R programming.
